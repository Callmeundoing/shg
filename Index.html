SAVE THE COLUMBIA RIVER GORGE





The Issue

Now, more than ever, the impact of the Bonneville, John Day, And Dalles dams extend farther than their desired functions, creating a quarrel between stakeholders on what should happen to the dams. The result of the impact ripples through every aspect. As the benefits might include lower electricity costs, some of the costs will be that tribes lose their sacred fishing grounds. As we look back at the importance of preserving the Bonneville, John Day, and Dalles Dams, we must factor in all the perspectives and highlight the need for urgent solutions that provide for everyone.







Our Solution






How Can You Help?

Spread the word! It's important that we make sure that people are aware that dams are a major problem in our day and age. Reach out to family members, friends, and even coworkers and inform them whats happening on the Columbia River Gorge! Reaching out to people can be done in serveral different ways, choose which one is right for you.


Stay informed! Staying informed about the most recent changes about the Columbia River Gorge Dams is crucial for spreading the correct information about how dams are affecting everyone no matter the benefits.




Our goal is to make sure that everyone gets a fair and equal stake in the dam.
