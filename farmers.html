The Sacrifices & Benefits

As a farmer who utilizes the lower Columbia River, we want to preserve the dams because dams provide water for our stock and irrigation. Even as the summer heats up us farmers will have a steady water supply because of the dams. Though there are no major benefits for us, an increase in salmon population will greatly help the community. The modification does not harm us if we have a stable and reliable source of irrigation.













What The Modification Means To Us

For farmers, modification doesn't affect us directly as the dam already provides us with water for stock and irrigation and the modification will not affect this flow. The salmon population will help the community as a whole, including us, but isnt helping us directly.
