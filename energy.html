The Sacrifices & Benefits

As a director of the energy company, we want to preserve the dam because it provides lots of income from electricity. However, modifying the dams would provide the same outcome. We benefit from it because we will still be making a stable income and will be able to help the environment. Possible disadvantages of modifying the dams include spending money on covering things such as design, equipment, and environmental checks. Overall, considering the costs and benefits this is a suitable solution.













What The Modification Means To Us

Modification of the dam will cost us a great sum of money for the changes, but it will still allow us to collect income from electricity. It will also remove all the backlash and comment we are receiving from the stakeholders. However, the modification can result in lower budget that can be spent on other equipment, environmental checks, and design changes. In conclusion, the benefits outweigh the costs, making this a viable solution.
